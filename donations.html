Acts of Kindness: Making a Difference in Manchester

At Acts of Kindness, we are dedicated to supporting Manchester's homeless community. We rely on the generosity of people like you to continue our mission. Every donation makes a real difference in the lives of those in need.

Here's why your support matters:

  • 100% of your donation goes directly to programs that benefit the homeless community. Acts of Kindness is a non-profit organization, and our board members volunteer their time and resources.
  • Your contribution provides vital opportunities and support. We offer a helping hand to those who need it most, fostering a brighter future for Manchester's homeless population.

We welcome all donations, big or small. Even a modest contribution can make a significant impact.
